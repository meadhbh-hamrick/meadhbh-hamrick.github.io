It occurs to me the reason I don't really believe in UFOs is the number of times I hear people on TV say things like "THE EVIDENCE IS OUT THERE. DECIDE FOR YOURSELF." You never hear the government say things like "THE EVIDENCE PAYING TAXES IS IMPORTANT TO SOCIETY IS OUT THERE. DECIDE FOR YOURSELF." No... they say "Pay your taxes or we'll throw you in jail." That's how I know paying taxes is important to the government, but UFOs aren't. #ufo [permalink]
